## Background

I am Tran Manh Dat, an AI Software Engineer and Researcher with a passion for building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. My career spans computer vision, automatic speech recognition, and Linux systems research, with production deployments ranging from embedded devices to cloud-scale APIs serving hundreds of concurrent users.

After graduating with a Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology from Le Quy Don Technical University (top 2% of my cohort), I worked across multiple Vietnamese technology companies developing AI-powered products. In 2024 I enrolled in a Master's programme in Information Technology at Chung-Ang University, Seoul, Korea, where I conducted research on Linux kernel storage subsystems and AI workload I/O performance. I left the programme after one semester as the research direction did not align with my background and goals.

I have co-authored several peer-reviewed publications in computer vision, speech processing, and file systems, and I am fluent in Vietnamese and English (IELTS 6.5).
